Doctor's Tips

What to track and why it matters.

📋 Why Log Everything?

  • Patterns help identify headache type (migraine, tension, cluster, etc.)
  • Triggers help prevent future episodes
  • Medication logs prevent overuse headache

🍽 Food Triggers

  • Log everything eaten 24 hours before a headache
  • Common: aged cheese, chocolate, alcohol, processed meats, MSG
  • Skipped meals and dehydration are very common triggers
  • Caffeine: too much AND withdrawal can both cause headaches

😴 Sleep Matters

  • Both too little AND too much sleep can trigger headaches
  • Sleep position affects neck tension — key for your neck/throat pain
  • Log sleep quality alongside your episodes

🏃 Activity & Posture

  • Prolonged screen time and poor posture are major triggers
  • Note if headache came after physical exertion
  • Driving and reading can strain neck muscles

💊 Medication Tracking

  • Note exact name and dose of every medication taken
  • Using pain relievers more than 2–3x/week can cause rebound headaches
  • Aceclofenac: take as prescribed (2x/day) — do not increase on your own

🔴 Seek Immediate Help If:

  • Sudden "worst headache of your life"
  • Headache with fever, stiff neck, confusion, or rash
  • Headache after head injury
  • Vision, speech, or limb problems with headache
  • Increasing throat tightness or difficulty breathing
